Gourmet Lemon Rosemary Chicken

restaurant Servings 4 People
bar_chart Difficulty Easy

Preparation

01

Marinate the Protein

In a large bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, and chopped rosemary. Place the chicken breasts in the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

02

Sear for Texture

Heat a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat. Place the chicken breasts skin-side down and sear for 5-6 minutes until golden brown and crispy. Flip the chicken and cook for another 2 minutes.

03

Bake with Citrus

Arrange the thinly sliced lemons around and on top of the chicken. Transfer the skillet to a preheated oven at 200°C (400°F). Roast for 15-20 minutes until the internal temperature reaches 75°C.

04

Rest and Serve

Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing. This ensures the juices redistribute for maximum tenderness. Garnish with fresh rosemary.

Chef's Notes

For a deeper flavor profile, add a splash of dry white wine to the skillet before transferring it to the oven. It creates a beautiful deglazed sauce that pairs perfectly with roasted potatoes or a light spring salad.
